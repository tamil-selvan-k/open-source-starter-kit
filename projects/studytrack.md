13: ## 💡 About the Project
14: StudyTrack is designed to transform academic chaos into peak productivity. It features an AI-powered tutor that explains complex concepts, a study tracker to build consistent habits, a GPA predictor, and a gamified progress dashboard. It also includes a deep focus room to help students beat procrastination.
